Landlines is a two-year cultural initiative developed by M12 STUDIO. The initiative will stimulate collaborative creative projects between M12 STUDIO and visiting artists, researchers, and writers who will spend time in Conejos County, Colorado. The collaborations will partner with ranchers and other landowners in the area to create site-specific artworks, events, and other creative projects. The research, events, and artworks produced will draw on a range of interests and expertise including; architecture, agriculture, photography, physiography, and contemporary and traditional village life.
